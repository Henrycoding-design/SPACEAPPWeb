Roadmap & Availability

v3.0 (Open-Source: Free) is the basic version of SPACEAPP and will remain available until December 2025 before being retired in favor of the upcoming v5.x releases.

v4.0 (Open-Source: Charged) is the plus version of SPACEAPP, with more features and improvements.

v4.2 (Closed-Source) is the national-supported version of SPACEAPP, with all the features of v4.0 plus some bug fixes and performance improvements.

v5.0 (Closed-Source) is SPACEAPP's best version: higher reliability and adaptability — cleaner logs, fewer API interruptions, and a self-updating satellite list and user location for better accuracy.
